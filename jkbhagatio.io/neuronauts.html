Neuronauts


Neuronauts is a fully-funded academic summer camp and school club I created in 2022. As an educational outreach initiative, Neuronauts aims to encourage secondary school students in the Greater London area to consider and pursue STEM degrees and careers. Neuronauts is hosted at the Sainsbury Wellcome Centre, and focuses on teaching students basics in Neuroscience, Computer Science, and Electrical Engineering, while allowing them to pursue creative projects in this space.

If you or a student you know is interested in participating, or you are interested in contributing, please get in contact with me!
